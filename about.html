about me

Software Developer & Web Developer Student

Based in Laredo, Texas

I grew up with that age-old, soul-searching question: “What do I want to be when I grow up?” For me, the answer came early—fueled by endless hours of button-mashing with my dad on the PlayStation One. At the wise age of 12, I made a bold declaration: “I’m going to be a video game developer!” Fast-forward a few years—and several reality checks later—I discovered that the gaming industry isn’t all boss battles and loot boxes. Turns out, getting an AA game out there is… well, a quest worthy of a side quest. So I pivoted. Digging deeper into what I truly enjoyed, I realized that building software, websites, and mobile apps had a lot in common with game development—minus the dragons (usually). And just like that, I found my groove. Today, you’ll find me debugging like a digital exterminator, patching lines of code like a pixelated hero, and loving every step of the journey.
